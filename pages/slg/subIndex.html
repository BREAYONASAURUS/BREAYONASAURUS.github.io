Our founding (Nationally & Gamma Chapter)

Sigma Lambda Gamma National Sorority, Inc. was founded on April 9th, 1990 at the University of Iowa in Iowa City Iowa. Gamma Chapter was founded at Iowa State University on April 11th, 1992.
